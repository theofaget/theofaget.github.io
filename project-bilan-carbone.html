Project Bilan Carbone

Formula Student Carbon Analysis

Rank: 8th Place

Introduction

This project aims to analyze the carbon footprint of our Formula Student car, providing insights into the environmental impact of our design and manufacturing processes.

Objectives

  • Calculate the total carbon emissions associated with the car.
  • Identify key areas for improvement in carbon reduction.
  • Benchmark against industry standards and competitors.

Methodology

We will use lifecycle assessment methods to quantify the carbon emissions, including material sourcing, production, transportation, and end-of-life disposal.

Results

Our analysis indicates that we ranked 8th in our category, highlighting the effectiveness of our strategies and areas where further improvement is possible.
